Food & Recovery Guide

❌ Avoid Foods

  • Oily & Deep Fried Food
  • Spicy Chilies
  • Sugary Sodas
  • Processed Junk Food

✅ Healthy Foods

  • Fresh Fruits (Citrus/Papaya)
  • Green Leafy Vegetables
  • Hydrating Fluids (Coconut Water)
  • Light Meals (Porridge/Moong Dal)

Daily Meal Plan

Morning: Herbal Tea & Fruit Bowl
Afternoon: Steamed Rice, Dal & Curd
Night: Vegetable Soup & 1 Roti
Tips for Management: 1)Eat small, frequent meals rather than heavy, infrequent ones.
2)Include antioxidants like berries and green leafy vegetables in the diet.
3)Prioritize rest and stay hydrated.
