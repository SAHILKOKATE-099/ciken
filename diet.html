Understanding Chikungunya

Prevention is better than cure.

How it Spreads

Chikungunya is a viral disease transmitted to humans by infected mosquitoes—specifically the Aedes aegypti and Aedes albopictus species. These mosquitoes typically bite during daylight hours.

Symptom Checker

⚠️ DO NOT IGNORE: If symptoms persist, visit a doctor immediately.
